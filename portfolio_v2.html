My Story,

It started with curiosity — why does that button feel satisfying to click? That question sent me down a rabbit hole of design systems, physics-based animations, and the obsessive pursuit of crafting interfaces that feel alive.

I'm a creative technologist who believes the gap between design and engineering is where the most interesting work happens. I don't just build products — I compose experiences where every micro-interaction, every easing curve, every typographic choice is intentional.

From cinematic birthday websites to data-heavy dashboards, I bring the same obsessive attention to every pixel. My superpower? Making complex things feel effortless and simple things feel remarkable.
